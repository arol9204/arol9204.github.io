Electric Vehicles in Washington State, US

A Tableau story with different dashboards about the EV population growth in Washington.

Project Details

The US Department of Energy made significant effort in battery development which resulted in 50% cost of the battery and within 4 years more affordable EVs were made. Today, consumers now have multitude of choices when buying EVs ranging from Hybrids, plug-in-hybrids, and electric across the world. This project will help to explore the growth rate of EV cars in Washington state while also exploring the performance of electric vehicles among diverse Manufacturers.

The project work aims to provide insights into the following:

1. Explore the growth rate of EV cars growing in Washington State?

  • What are the best-selling EV Manufacturer and models?
  • Which manufacturer dominates the different cities in Washington State?
  • Differentiating factors for the all-electric or plug-in hybrid vehicles per Manufacturer and city.
  • Which brand identifies more with all-electric, plug-in hybrid vehicles, or a combination of both?
  • Which cities in Washington state have the highest number of electric vehicles?

    • 2. Explore the performance of electric vehicles among the diverse Manufacturers?

  • Compare the electric range among different manufacturers.
  • Compare the speed performance among different manufacturers.
  • Compare Fast charging rate among different manufacturers.
  • Compare the retail prices vs the efficiency.

    • 3. Provides a guide for potential buyers of EVs
